Monroe Unveiled: A Digital Edition

"Marilyn Unveiled: A Digital Edition" stands as a paramount digital resource dedicated to exploring the multifaceted legacy of Marilyn Monroe, a figure of immense cultural and cinematic significance. This digital edition serves not merely as a repository but as a portal into the essence of Marilyn Monroe, presenting a comprehensive collection that spans her personal writings, artistic endeavors, and her indelible impact on film and popular culture. The project aims to provide an enriched understanding of Marilyn's life, beyond her public persona, through her personal letters, reflective poems, and artistic drawings, thereby offering a nuanced glimpse into her internal landscape.

The influence of Marilyn Monroe extends beyond her personal artifacts, shaping popular culture and the cinematic landscape. This digital edition meticulously chronicles her filmography, underscoring her versatility as an actress and her timeless contributions to the arts. Furthermore, the edition employs the Text Encoding Initiative (TEI) for a scholarly examination of Marilyn's works, employing a structured digital framework to facilitate a detailed analysis of her written and artistic output.

In the section Introduction, visitors will find a comprehensive overview of the digital edition and an evolving discussion on the editorial principles guiding this project.

The Materials section aggregates essential resources, including a bibliography of texts and a filmography detailing Monroe's cinematic journey.

The project's technical backbone is rooted in XML, adhering to the Text Encoding Initiative (TEI) standards. All textual data are disseminated under an ‘open access’ license, embodying the principles of ‘open science’ by allowing for the expansion and enhancement of the project's materials.

We invite you to delve into "Marilyn Unveiled: A Digital Edition," an endeavor to traverse the life, writings, and cinematic legacy of Marilyn Monroe. This edition promises a journey of discovery into the depth and breadth of an iconic figure's enduring influence.
